Frequently Asked Questions

What are the most important things I should know about riding a bike?

Safety First! Always obey the rules of the road. Obey all traffic signals, signs, and laws. Get in the mindset of “driving” your bike—not just “riding” your bike. This will help you be a more focused and legally compliant bike rider.

How can I tell if my helmet is old and I need a new one?

Check your helmet for cracks, dents, loose padding, faded labels, or after five years of regular use, replace it.

My bike has been in storage is it safe to ride?

Check tires, brakes, and chain for wear or rust; test fluids and battery (if applicable), and ensure everything functions smoothly before riding after storage..

What rules should I follow when riding my bike? ans in 30 words

Follow traffic laws, wear safety gear, stay visible, signal turns, avoid distractions, ride in bike lanes, and check equipment before riding to ensure safety and compliance.
